Beading Safety Guide
👶
Not for children under 3 Small parts pose choking hazard. Parental supervision required.
🙅
Do not put beads in mouth, nose or ears Use only for crafting patterns. Not a snack or toy for holes.
👪
Parents should accompany during crafting Count together, learn colors - fun and safe!
📦
Store beads in container after play Prevent rolling on floor to avoid choking hazards.
🔥
Ironing Instructions
Avoid painter's tape when ironing. Iron directly on bead boards.
⚙️
Tool Recommendations
Avoid low-quality irons. Heat press recommended.
🪑 Recommended Crafting Posture
● Keep proper distance from bead work. Don't get too close.
● Sit straight with feet flat. No slouching.
● Do not lie down, lie on stomach, or lean while crafting.
👀 Recommended Crafting Duration
● Limit crafting sessions to 20 minutes max.
● Take outdoor breaks to rest eyes after crafting.
